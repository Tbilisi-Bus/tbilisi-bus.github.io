Tbilisi Bus
ABOUT

Tbilisi Bus is urban mobility app. Here is information about all local transports: Bus, Minibus, Metro.

• Check the routes for Tbilisi transports.

• Stop board info and schedules in real time.

• Bookmark favorite bus stops.

• Check live bus locations.

• Search certain transports and stops.

• Tbilisi Bus is available in Georgian and English languages.

What is personal information?

Personal information is information about a person, namely information that identifies an individual or may with reasonable efforts or together with additional information we have access to, enable the identification of an individual, or information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person which may be of a private or sensitive nature. Under certain applicable laws identification of an individual also includes any information which associates an individual with a persistent identifier such as a name, an identification number, persistent cookie identifier etc.

Personal information does not include information that has been anonymized or aggregated so that it can no longer identify or be used to identify a specific natural person.

What and when do we collect personal information?

We collect personal information from you and from any devices (including mobile devices) you use when you install or have the App installed (or pre-installed) on your device, or when you use or access our Services, provide us with information such as your favourites and travel habits.

Personal information we collect automatically when you use our Services:

Tbilisi Bus automatically collects location in order to provide you with our Services.

In addition, you may choose to upload specific destinations and POIs such as your home address, work address or other favourite destinations and POIs in order to personalize the Service.

We use Google Analytics which tracks user interactions on buttons in Tbilisi Bus application, this is used for only statistics purpose for us to determine what we have to improve in our app for users.

We save your information into your device (not anywhere else), such as favorite bus stops to make it easier for you to use the application.
